About IPL

History

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, founded in 2008.

Tournament Format

Eight teams compete in a round-robin league followed by playoffs, leading to the IPL final.

Rules

Each match is 20 overs per side. Standard T20 rules apply, including Powerplays and Super Overs.
